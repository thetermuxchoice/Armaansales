Automation

We have developed our own 90% automated software which serves as the perfect solution. Besides ensuring that everything can be actioned and managed from a singular access point, we also streamline time-consuming processes, which is one of the primary reasons for starting DropShipping businesses in the first place.

Product Selection:

The struggle to find products that appeal to customers and actually sell is one of the most frustrating aspects of running an eCommerce store. This process is made seamless with Dropsys' software. We allow you to choose from millions of products and simply add them to their store(s) at the click of a button.

Orders:

When it comes to DropShipping, processing orders isn't always straightforward. In order to fulfil the customer's order, initial payments must be taken, and orders must be placed with manufacturers/suppliers. By using Dropsys' 90% automated software, you are able to streamline this entire process by managing and tracking everything from one central location.

Tracking:

When a customer places an order, it is forwarded to the manufacturer and fulfilment centre, which then ships it to the customer's selected location. Throughout this process, the product must be tracked to provide updates to both the store owner and the customer, ensuring delivery times are met. The 90% automation software allows you to manage your orders from a central location, updating orders with tracking information, and ensuring peace of mind for your customers.

Returns:

There is no store owner that enjoys dealing with returns, but they are a part of eCommerce that every store owner has to deal with at some point. We have a significantly lower return rate than most eCommerce shops at 5%-15%, compared to 30% for most eCommerce stores. We have a low return rate due to the quality of our products and the quality of our services. By providing a central access point to store owners, our automation software simplifies and streamlines the entire process, allowing everyone to communicate clearly and consistently.

Replacements:

Occasionally, a customer discovers an issue with a product after receiving it. The ability to track orders and trace the steps of that order through a single central access point not only simplifies tracking orders, but also isolates the possible cause of any issues. If a replacement item must be issued to a customer, our automated software will have the entire process already mapped out, allowing follow-up orders to be carried out seamlessly.
