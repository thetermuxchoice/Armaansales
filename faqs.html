Why dropship in the first place?

With dropshipping you can purchase and sell large quantities of products without having to hold any inventory in storage. In addition to this, it provides business owners an unparalleled level of freedom and flexibility, allowing them to decide when, where and how they want to work.

What Advantages Does Dropshipping Offer?

The most obvious advantage is the lower overhead expenses when compared to traditional brick and mortar stores. No need for storage space or inventory, just a computer and Internet access. This in turn means no packaging or shipping costs.

It is also less risky as you will only need to buy a product after your customer has already bought it from you, thus removing the risk of your supply surpassing demand and damaging profit margins.

What Is The Profitability Of Dropshipping?

Most clients dropshipping stores don’t even need to generate $1000 in sales before profits start to roll in. The more SKUs you have listed, across more markets, the greater these profits will be.

What Level Of Growth Can Be Expected?

The growth potential is up to you. Some clients are only looking for a bit of additional income, thus are happy working only 30 min to an hour per day, and letting the business grow at a steadier pace. Meanwhile others seek to accelerate this process in the hopes that it can potentially become their primary source of income. Therefore they are willing to put in 5 to 6 hours per day.
